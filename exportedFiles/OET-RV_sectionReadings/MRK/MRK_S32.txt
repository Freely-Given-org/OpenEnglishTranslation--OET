Today we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 6 45 to 52.
The section heading is: Yeshua walks on the lake surface.


Straight after that, Yeshua sent his apprentices off in the boat to go across to Bethsaida while he sent the crowd home. Then he said goodbye to everyone, and climbed the hill to pray. Later in the night, the boat was out in the middle of the lake, and only Yeshua remained back there on the land. He saw them struggling to row against the wind, and sometime after 3am he came out to them, walking on the lake surface as if to walk past them. But they had seen someone walking on the lake and thought it was a ghost, and they yelled out because they had all seen it and were terrified.

But Yeshua immediately spoke to them and said, “Hey, take heart, it's me, don't be afraid!” Then he climbed up into the boat and the wind died down. And they were all totally astounded because they hadn't understood the implications of him feeding all the people because their minds were closed.

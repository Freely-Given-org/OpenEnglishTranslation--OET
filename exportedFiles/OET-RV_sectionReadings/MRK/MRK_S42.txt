Next we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 8 27 to 30.
The section heading is: Yeshua asks who they think he is.


Then Yeshua and his followers moved around the various villages of Caesaria Philippi. On the way, he asked them, “Who does everyone think I am?”

They said, “Some say Yohan-the-immerser, some say Eliyah, and some say one of the prophets.”

Then he asked, “And what do you all think?”

Peter said, “You are the promised one sent by God.”

Then Yeshua warned them not to tell others about him.

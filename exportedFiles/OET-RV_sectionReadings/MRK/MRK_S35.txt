Today we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 7 14 to 23.
The section heading is: Evil comes from inside a person.


Yeshua called out to the crowd, “Everyone listen to me carefully: Nothing that enters your mouth makes you guilty in God's eyes. It's what comes out of you that demonstrates your culpability.”

◘ When Yeshua came back inside, his apprentices asked him what he meant. But he scolded them, “Can't you understand something so simple! Can't you see that anything from the outside that enters your body doesn't make you guilty in God's sight, because it doesn't enter your mind but merely goes into your stomach and ends up in the toilet.” (By saying this he clearly showed that is was ok for Jews to eat any food.)

He continued, “It's what comes out of you that makes you guilty in God's eyes. That's because evil thoughts, sexual immorality, theft, murder, adultery, greed, wickedness, deceit, arrogance, lust, slander, pride, and foolishness all come from inside people's hearts and minds. So it's the evil that's inside a person and comes out that makes them guilty in God's eyes.”

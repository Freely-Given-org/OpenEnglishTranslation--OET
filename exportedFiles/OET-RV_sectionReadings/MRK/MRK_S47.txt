Next we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 9 33 to 37.
The section heading is: How to be the best.


Then they went to Capernaum and when they arrived at the house, Yeshua asked them what they were discussing on the way.

But they didn't reply because while they were walking, they had been talking about which of them is the best. So Yeshua sat down and called the twelve of them and explained, “Anyone who wants to be at the top, has to be at the bottom and help all the others.” Then Yeshua called a young boy and stood him in the middle of them, and giving him a hug said, “Whoever welcomes young children on my behalf, welcomes me, and anyone who welcomes me is not actually welcoming me, but welcoming the one who sent me here.”

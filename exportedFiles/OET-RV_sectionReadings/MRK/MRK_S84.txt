Right now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 15 1 to 5.
The section heading is: Pilate tries to interrogate Yeshua.


First thing in the morning, the chief priests called a meeting with the elders and the religious teachers and all the council, and they ordered that Yeshua be tied up and taken over to Governor Pilate. There Pilate asked him, “Are you the king of the Jews?”

“You said it,” replied Yeshua.

Then the chief priests started hurling accusations against Yeshua, so Pilate asked him again, “Don't you have anything to say? Listen to all these accusations they're making against you.”

But Pilate was astounded that Yeshua didn't answer any further.

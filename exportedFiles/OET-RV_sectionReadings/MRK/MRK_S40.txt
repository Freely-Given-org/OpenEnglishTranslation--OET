Now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 8 14 to 21.
The section heading is: The apprentices miss the significance of what’s happening.


But Yeshua's apprentices forgot to take any food other than one bun that someone in the boat had. He told them, “Watch out for the yeast of the Pharisees and of King Herod.”

They asked each other what it meant—was it because they forgot food?

But Yeshua knew what they were saying and asked, “Why are you all discussing about not having food? Can't you figure it out? Or are your minds completely closed? You all have eyes but can't seem to see and have ears but can't seem to listen. Have you already forgotten what just happened? When I broke up the five buns to feed five thousand people, how many basketfuls of leftovers did you collect?”

“Twelve,” they answered.

“And when we fed four thousand with seven buns, how many full baskets of leftovers then?”

“Seven,” they answered.

Yeshua asked, “How can you all not understand what's going on right in front of you!”

Now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 1 9 to 13.
The section heading is: Yeshua gets immersed and then tempted.


Then one day Yeshua came from Nazareth in the Galilee region, and he got immersed in the Yordan river by Yohan. As Yeshua came up out of the water, he saw the sky being opened up, and God's spirit coming down on him like a dove. And a voice came from the sky saying, “You are my son who I love and am pleased with.”

And then the spirit sent him into the wilderness where he was tested by Satan for forty days. He was out there with the wild animals, but messengers waited on him there.

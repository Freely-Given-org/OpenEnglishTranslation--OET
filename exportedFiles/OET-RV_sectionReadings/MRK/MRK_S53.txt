Today we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 10 32 to 34.
The section heading is: Yeshua predicts his execution a third time.


They were heading south on their way to Yerushalem with Yeshua in front, and his followers were a bit surprised and a bit scared. Yeshua took the twelve aside and started telling them what was going to happen to him, “We're on our way down to Yerushalem and humanity's child will be turned over to the chief priests and the religious teachers, and they'll sentence him to die and pass him over to the non-Jews. Then they'll ridicule him and spit on him before they beat him and execute him, but he'll come back to life again in three days time.”

Later again when the eleven apprentices were sitting around eating, Yeshua appeared in the room and scolded them for not believing and for being so stubborn when they hadn't believed others who had told them that he'd come back from the dead. Then he instructed them, “Travel around all the world and tell everyone the good message. Anyone who believes and gets immersed in water will be saved, however, those who refuse to believe will be condemned to death. Those who believe will be able to do miracles: they'll be able to use my authority to command demons to leave and to speak new languages, and they'll be able to pick up snakes. If they drink anything poisonous, it won't hurt them, and they will place their hands on people and make them better.”

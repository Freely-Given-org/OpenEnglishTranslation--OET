The section heading is: Yeshua asks about the messiah being a descendant of David.


Later when Yeshua was teaching in the temple, he asked the people, “How is it that the teachers here say that the messiah is a descendant of King David? Because David himself said under the inspiration of the holy spirit, ‘The master told my master, “Sit there in the position of honour until I can conquer your enemies.” ’ If David calls the messiah his master, how can the messiah possibly be David's descendant?”

The large crowd was very interested to hear everything that Yeshua had to say.

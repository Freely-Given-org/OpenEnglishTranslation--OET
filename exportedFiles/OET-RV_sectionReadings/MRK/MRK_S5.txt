Right now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 1 14 to 20.
The section heading is: The selection of the first followers/apprentices.


After Yohan had been arrested, Yeshua went back up to Galilee announcing the good news from God saying, “The promised time is here, and God's kingdom is now near. Turn from your sins and believe this good news.”

As he went around the lake of Galilee, Yeshua saw two fishermen, Simon and his brother Andrew throwing a net into the lake. And he said to them, “Come with me and I will make you into fishermen of people.” And so they left their fishing work and went off with Yeshua.

Then a bit further on he saw two of Zebedee's sons, Yacob and his brother Yohan, in a boat getting their nets ready. Yeshua called them and they too went off with him, leaving their father Zebedee in the boat with the hired workers.

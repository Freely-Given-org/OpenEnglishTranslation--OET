Now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 2 23 to 28.
The section heading is: Working on the Rest Day.


And it so happened that Yeshua and his followers passed through some fields of grain on some days of rest. As they went through, they plucked off some heads of grain. And some members of the Pharisee party said, “It's breaking the law to harvest grain on a rest day!”

And Yeshua replied, “You've read yourselves what David did when him and his men were hungry. They went into God's tent and ate the holy bread representing the presence of God. Only the priests were allowed to eat that, and yet David even gave some to his men!” And he told the Pharisees, “The days of rest were created for people, not people for the rest days. So I, humanity's child, have authority also over the day of rest.”

The section heading is: The religious leaders want to see a miracle.


Some men from the Pharisee party came out to Yeshua and started arguing with him. They were checking him out and wanted to see a miracle. Yeshua grieved inside and asked, “Why do you people need to see a miracle before you'll believe me? Actually, no miracle will be done for this generation.”

So Yeshua left in the boat again and crossed to the other side of the lake.

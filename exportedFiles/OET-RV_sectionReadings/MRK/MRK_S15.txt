The section heading is: Big crowds press on Yeshua.


After that, Yeshua withdrew towards the lake with his followers, and a huge crowd of people from the regions of both Galilee and down in Yudea, along with others from Yerushalem, Idumea, from across the Yordan River, and from up near Tyre and Tsidon. They came because they had heard about the amazing things he had done. So Yeshua asked his apprentices to organise a small boat to be there for him so he wouldn't be cramped by the press of the crowd. Virtually anyone who had any physical issues was pressing towards him to touch him, because he had already healed so many. And the demons, whenever they saw Yeshua, they fell down to the ground in front of him and yelled out, “You are God's son!” But he regularly warned them not to reveal who he was.

Next we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 16 9 to 11.
The section heading is: The others don’t believe that Maria saw Yeshua.


After coming back to life early on Sunday morning, Yeshua appeared first to Maria from Magdala. (She was the one that he had once commanded seven demons to leave.) After leaving him, she went and told his followers who were mourning and weeping at his loss. But when she told them that he is alive again and that she'd seen him, they simply didn't believe her.

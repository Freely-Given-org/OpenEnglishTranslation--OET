Right now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 11 15 to 19.
The section heading is: Yeshua upsets the traders inside the temple.


Then they came back into Yerushalem and on into the temple where Yeshua started driving out the sellers and the buyers that were trading in there. Then he tipped over the tables of the money-changers and the chairs of the ones selling doves, and stopped others from carrying goods through the temple. He said, “Isn't it written in the Scriptures, ‘My house will be known as a house of prayer for all the nations’? But all of you have turned it into a sanctuary for swindlers.”

When the chief priests and the religious teachers heard this, they started looking for a way that they could destroy him, because they were afraid of him and the way that the crowd marvelled at his teaching.

Every evening, Yeshua and his apprentices would go out of the city to stay overnight.

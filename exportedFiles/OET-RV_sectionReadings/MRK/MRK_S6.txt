Now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 1 21 to 28.
The section heading is: Yeshua drives out a demon.


They went on into Capernaum where he went into the meeting hall and began teaching as soon as the rest day started. The congregation was amazed at his teaching, because he taught with authority, not like the regular teachers there.

Then a demon-possessed man in the meeting hall suddenly yelled out, “What are we to you, Yeshua of Nazareth? You've come to destroy us. I know that you're God's holy one.”

But Yeshua scolded it, saying, “Keep quiet!”, and, “Come out of him!”

Then the demon threw the man into a convulsion, cried out loudly, and left him. Everyone was astounded and asked each other, “What just happened? We haven't heard anything like this before—he has authority to command demons, and they obey him!”

And the news about Yeshua spread all over the entire region of Galilee.

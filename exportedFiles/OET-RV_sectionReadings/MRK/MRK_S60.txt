Next we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 11 26 to 33.
The section heading is: The leaders question Yeshua’s authority.


◘ They continued on into Yerushalem and on to the temple where they were met by the chief priests and the religious teachers and the elders. They asked Yeshua, “Do you have any authority to do what you did? If so, who gave it to you?”

Yeshua answered, “Okay, I'll also ask you something and when you answer me I'll tell you about my authority to do that: When Yohan preached and immersed people, did the authority for that come from heaven or from people here?”

They discussed this among themselves, reasoning, “If we say it was from heaven, he'll ask us why we didn't believe him. But we can't say that it was just from people.” (They said that because they were scared of the crowds, knowing that they believed that Yohan was a prophet.) So they answered, “We don't know.”

And Yeshua also responded, “Well then, I won't tell you about my authority to do those things either.”

Then Yeshua continued his teaching, “All of you will stumble because it's written in the scriptures: ‘I'll strike down the shepherd and the sheep will be scattered.’ But after I come back to life, I'll go on ahead of you back to Galilee.”

But Peter contradicted him, “Even if everyone else stumbles, I won't!”

And Yeshua responded, “I can assure you that you'll renounce me three times this very night, before the rooster crows early tomorrow morning.”

But Peter argued forcibly, “Even if I had to die with you, I would never disown you!”

And all the others said similar things.

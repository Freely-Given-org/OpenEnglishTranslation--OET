The section heading is: Yeshua gives a lesson on moving mountains.


As they entered the city again in the morning, Yeshua's apprentices noticed that the fig tree was totally dead. Peter remembered what he had said to the tree, and commented, “Teacher, that fig tree that you cursed has withered up.”

Yeshua responded, “Use your faith in God. I can assure you that if anyone tells a mountain to go into the sea and doesn't doubt in their mind but believes that what they're saying will happen, then it will. So I tell you all that anything you ask God for in your prayers, if you believe that you have received it, then you will. Whenever you are standing praying, if you have something against someone, be sure to forgive them so that your father in heaven can also forgive your shortcomings.”

Next we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 8 22 to 26.
The section heading is: Yeshua heals a blind man near Bethsaida.


As they entered Bethsaida, the people brought a blind man to Yeshua and asked him to touch him. So Yeshua took the man's hand and led him out of the village. Then he spat in his eyes, placed his hands on him, and asked him, “What can you see?”

The man looked around and answered, “I can see the people, but they just look like trees.” Yeshua touched the man's eyes again and then he could see clearly—he was now completely better. Then Yeshua told him not to go back into the village but to go straight home.

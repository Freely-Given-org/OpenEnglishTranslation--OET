Right now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 1 40 to 45.
The section heading is: Yeshua heals a man with leprosy.


A man with leprosy came to Yeshua asking him, “I know that you could heal me if you wanted to.”

Yeshua felt compassion for him, reached out his hand and touched him, and said, “Yes, I will. Get well.” Then immediately the leprosy disappeared and he was well again. So Yeshua sent him away, but warned him: “Don't talk to anyone—just go to the priest and show him that you're well, and as a testimony of that, make the offering that Mosheh commanded.”

But after the man left, he started telling lots of people and spreading the news—so much so that Yeshua could no longer come into the town but had to stay out in quieter places, and people kept coming to him.

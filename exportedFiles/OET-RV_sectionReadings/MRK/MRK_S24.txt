Next we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 4 30 to 34.
The section heading is: The small seed produces a large result.


Again he said, “What could we liken God's kingdom to? Or what parable could we explain it with? It's like planting a mustard seed in the ground—a very small seed. But when it's planted and grows up, it forms a large bush with many branches so that birds can rest in its shade.”

And so as much as they could handle it, Yeshua shared the good message with them via many similar word pictures. He never spoke without these illustrations, but he explained all of them to his close followers.

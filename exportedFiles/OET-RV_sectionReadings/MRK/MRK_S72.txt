Right now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 13 28 to 31.
The section heading is: Watch out for the change in season.


“So learn a lesson from fig trees—whenever the branches green up and start sprouting leaves and then everyone knows that summer is close. So too whenever you see all these things happening, you know that the end of this current world is close. I can assure you that the generation of people who see these things won't die off before these latter things happen. The sky and the planet might collapse, but my teachings will never become irrelevant.

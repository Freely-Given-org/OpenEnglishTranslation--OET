Now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 6 53 to 56.
The section heading is: Yeshua heals many people around Gennesaret.


After completing their crossing, they anchored at Gennesaret. And when they got out of the boat, again people recognised Yeshua and ran around that region, so next minute people began bringing all of those who were sick, carrying them on pallets to wherever they heard that Yeshua was. And whenever Yeshua entered villages or cities or fields or marketplaces, they laid the sick down there and were imploring him that they might even just touch the edge of his robe, and anyone who did so was healed.

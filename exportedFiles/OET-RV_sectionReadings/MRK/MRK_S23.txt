Today we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 4 26 to 29.
The section heading is: The parable concerning the seed.


And then, “God's kingdom is like a person throwing some seed onto the ground. Whether the person sleeps at night or stays up night and day, the seed will sprout and grow either way, and they don't know how it does it. The earth is designed to multiply the seed: first a sprout appears, then a bigger plant, and then the full head of grain. Then when it's ripe, the harvester is sent in because it's ready for harvest.”

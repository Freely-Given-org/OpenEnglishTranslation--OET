Next we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 14 43 to 52.
The section heading is: Yeshua is arrested in the night.


Even while Yeshua was still speaking, Yudas reached him, followed by a crowd from the chief priests, the religious teachers, and the elders—many armed with swords and clubs. Yudas had prearranged a signal with them that anyone who he greeted with a kiss, they should grab that person and lead him away under guard.

So he walked right up to Yeshua and said, “My honoured teacher,” and greeted him with a kiss. Then the others with him grabbed Yeshua and secured him. But one of the ones with Yeshua that was beside him, drew his sword and struck a slave of the chief priest, cutting off the man's ear. Yeshua spoke to the crowd, “Did you all bring swords and clubs to try to capture me like you'd chase down a thief? I was standing there teaching in the temple every day and you could have arrested me, but it's happened this way to ensure that the what's written in scriptures will be fulfilled.”

Meanwhile, the others who'd been with Yeshua had taken off. One young fellow was wearing a single linen robe, and when the crowd tried to grab him, it came off in the struggle and he ran away stark naked.

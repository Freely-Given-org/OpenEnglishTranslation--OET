Right now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 16 1 to 8.
The section heading is: The massive shock at the burial chamber.


After the Rest Day was over, Maria from Magdala and Maria the mother of Yacob and Salome, went and bought some traditional burial spices that evening to take to the burial chamber and place with the body. Then very early on Sunday morning they went to the chamber, arriving just as the sun was rising. But they realised that they didn't have anyone with them to roll the rock away that was covering the entrance. However, when they looked they saw that the very large rock had already been rolled away. So they went on into the burial chamber and were totally stunned to find a young man dressed in white, sitting in there on the side.

He assured them, “Don't be surprised. I know you're looking for Yeshua from Nazareth, the one who was executed on a pole. But see there where his body was—he's not there because he came back to life. So you three should go and tell his apprentices, and Peter in particular, that he's going to go up ahead of them to Galilee and you'll all see him up there, just as he already told you.”

So they left the chamber and took off running, still stunned and shaking and too scared to stop and to talk to anyone.

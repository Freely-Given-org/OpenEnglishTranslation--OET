Right now we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 12 41 to 44.
The section heading is: The sacrificial donation of a widow.


As he sat down in front of the temple treasury, Yeshua watched the crowd as they threw coins into the dish, and some rich people were donating quite valuable silver coins. Then an impoverished widow came and threw in two small copper coins. Yeshua called his followers and told them, “Honestly, that poor widow gave more than all the others. You see, most people donate from their excess wealth, but this woman even gave some of the small amount that she needed to live on.”

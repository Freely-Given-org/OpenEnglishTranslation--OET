Today we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 1 2 to 8.
The section heading is: The ministry of Yohan the immerser (The return of ‘Eliyah’).


The prophet Yeshayah wrote: “See, I am sending my messenger to you, the one who will be preparing your path. The voice out in the wilderness shouting, ‘Get the path ready for Yahweh; Make it smooth for him to travel on.’ ”

Yohan turned up in the wilderness announcing that people should get immersed in water in order to show that their sins have been forgiven. And many from the Yudean region and from the city of Yerushalem went out to hear him, and they confessed their sins and asked Yohan to immerse them in the Yordan river.

Yohan was dressed in simple clothes made from camel hair with a leather belt around his waist, and he lived on locusts and wild honey. And he also announced, “There's a greater man coming soon—in fact I'm not even good enough to bend down and untie his sandals. I immersed you in water, but he will immerse you all in God's holy spirit.”

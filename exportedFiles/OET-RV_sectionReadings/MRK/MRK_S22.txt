Today we have a reading of a section from the Open English Translation of the bible, reading from the Readers Version Mark 4 21 to 25.
The section heading is: The light will shine on everything.


And Yeshua told them, “Obviously no one goes and gets a lamp, and then puts it under a basket or under the bed, but rather they place it somewhere higher on a stand. You see, nothing is hidden that won't eventually be disclosed, and there's no secret that won't eventually be revealed. Anyone who wants to understand must listen carefully.”

And he continued, “Take care what you listen to. Because how much you judge others, so too will judgement be handed out to you, and even more. Anyone who has faith will be given a reward, but if you have none, even the little you have will be taken away.”
